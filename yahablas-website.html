Role Designer & Developer
Year 2022
Status Live

About this Project

Ya Hablas Idiomas is a well-established language academy located in the heart of Alicante, Spain. Since 2010, they've been teaching over 15 European and Asian languages — English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, Korean, and more — through private lessons, group courses, corporate training, and school trip programs.

I designed and built their full marketing website using Webflow. The site is fully bilingual (Spanish & English), featuring a hero slider, detailed course catalog with language cards, corporate training section, school trip packages, a client logos carousel, WhatsApp live chat widget, registration form, and SEO optimization. The responsive design adapts seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
