About this Project

A bold, dark-themed website I designed and built for Pine Schneiderei und Stickerei — a tailoring and embroidery shop based in Magdeburg's City Carre. The brief was clear: an elegant, no-frills presence that lets the craft speak for itself. Black backgrounds, sharp serif headings, and stark photography create an atmosphere of precision and quality.

The site covers all service areas — alterations for every garment type, custom manufacturing, fur and leather work, and embroidery — with a fully transparent pricing section. Clean navigation, a focused contact section, and responsive layout ensure every visitor can quickly find what they need.
