Ya Hablas — Language Academy

A full-featured language learning platform with 7 languages, 6 levels, 15 exercise types, and 2,000+ questions per course

Role Designer & Developer
Year 2026
Status Live

About this Project

A comprehensive language learning platform I designed and built from scratch for Ya Hablas Idiomas. The app supports 7 languages across 6 proficiency levels each (A0–C1), with every course containing over 2,000 exercises organized into thematic units. It features 15 distinct exercise types — including Multiple Choice, True/False, Vocabulary Drill, Fill in the Blank, Matching, Opposites, Sentence Ordering, Minimal Pairs, Error Correction, and Dialogue — plus 5 practice modes: Speed Round, Listening, Speaking, Dictation, and Shadowing.

Each unit includes structured teaching materials and a study guide alongside the exercises. The platform features Duolingo-style gamification with XP progression, streak tracking, achievement badges, a competitive leaderboard, daily challenges, a personal word bank, spaced repetition, and text-to-speech pronunciation. The interface is fully bilingual (Spanish & English) with light/dark mode support and a warm, encouraging UX designed to keep learners engaged.
